About 关于我

👤

I'm a Game Designer based in London, specialising in systems design, game economy, and monetisation for free-to-play mobile games.

I have experience designing progression systems, resource economy models, gacha mechanics, and live ops events across multiple shipped F2P titles. I'm comfortable owning features from concept through documentation to implementation handoff, working closely with engineering and art teams.

Outside of work, I build AI-powered tools and automation systems, and I'm exploring the intersection of AI and game design.

Education

MA Game Design — UAL London College of Communication (2022–2023)
BE Engineering Geology — Jilin University (2015–2020)

我是一名居住在伦敦的游戏设计师，专注于 F2P 手游的系统设计、游戏经济和商业化设计。

我有多个已上线 F2P 项目的经验，涵盖成长系统、资源经济模型、抽卡机制和运营活动设计。能独立负责从概念、文档到落地的全流程，与程序和美术紧密协作。

工作之余，我在构建 AI 驱动的工具和自动化系统，探索 AI 与游戏设计的交叉领域。
