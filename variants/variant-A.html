About 关于我
[ photo ]

Hi, I'm Ziwei. 你好，我是子薇。

I'm a game designer specializing in systems design, economy balancing, and monetisation strategy. I hold an MA in Game Design and have shipped titles across idle, RPG, and shooter genres. 我是一名游戏策划，专注于系统设计、经济平衡和商业化策略。拥有游戏设计硕士学位，参与过放置、RPG 和射击品类的上线项目。

Beyond game design, I explore the intersection of AI and creative tools — building multi-agent systems and generative pipelines as side projects. I believe great games are built on robust systems, not just good ideas. 工作之余，我探索 AI 与创意工具的交叉领域，搭建多 Agent 系统和生成式管线。我相信好游戏源于扎实的系统设计，而非仅凭灵感。
