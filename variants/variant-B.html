About关于我

I am a game designer with a focus on systems, economy, and monetisation for free-to-play titles. My work spans idle games, card RPGs, and shooter hybrids, always centred on creating loops that players find genuinely compelling.

Beyond professional projects, I explore AI-driven content pipelines and multi-agent systems, believing that the intersection of game design thinking and emerging technology is where the most interesting work happens.

I hold an MA in Game Design and bring a blend of analytical rigour and creative intuition to every project I touch.

我是一名游戏策划，专注于F2P游戏的系统、经济和商业化设计。工作涵盖挂机游戏、卡牌RPG和射击混合品类，始终以打造玩家真正沉浸的核心循环为目标。

在职业项目之外，我还探索AI驱动的内容管线和多智能体系统，相信游戏设计思维与新兴技术的交汇处藏着最有趣的机会。

我拥有游戏设计硕士学位，将分析严谨性与创意直觉融入每一个项目。
