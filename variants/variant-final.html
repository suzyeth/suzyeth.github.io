About关于我

Hi, I'm Ziwei.你好，我是紫薇。

👤

I'm a Game Designer based in London, specialising in systems design, game economy, and monetisation for free-to-play mobile games.

I've designed progression systems, resource economy models, gacha mechanics, and live ops events across multiple shipped F2P titles. I own features from concept through documentation to implementation handoff.

Outside of work, I build AI-powered tools and automation systems, exploring the intersection of AI and game design.

我是一名居住在伦敦的游戏设计师，专注于 F2P 手游的系统设计、游戏经济和商业化。

有多个已上线项目的经验，涵盖成长系统、资源经济、抽卡机制和运营活动。能独立负责从概念到落地的全流程。

工作之余，构建 AI 驱动的工具和自动化系统，探索 AI 与游戏设计的交叉领域。
